Original Mizutaki

Japanese Dish

Mizutaki is a kind of hot pot dish in which fish or other kinds of meat and vegetables are cooked in unseasoned fish broth and dipped in tangy Ponzu sauce. It is somewhat similar to Sukiyaki. But Mizutaki is an even more perfect food for cold weather.

Sauce for Mizutaki

Cook Time: 15 minutes

1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1/4 cup citrus juice (such as lemon or Yuzu)
