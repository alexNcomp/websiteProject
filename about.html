About Yoko's Kitchen

Here at Yoko's Kitchen we strive to provide a catering service that you will love with cuisine that would have everyone coming to your events. Weither its a party or a simple get together, we at Yoko believe in providing the best food for the occasion. We also strive to train Chefs in not only the proper techniques for preparing Japanese cuisine but we strive to give them a foundation on which they reach greater heights in their careers as Chefs. Our business allows people who are interested in learning Japanese cuisine to take courses to learn how to make dishes like Yakitori,Tsukune,Okonomiyaki and Mizutaki. Future interns will receive payment for the efforts and hard work they show to make not only thmeslves improve, but making Yoko a more welcoming and enriching experience.

We hope that you can achieve your goal with the services we provide weither it is to learn new skills or have a amazing experience. Our company aims to provide and support all our users become the best version of themsleves.
