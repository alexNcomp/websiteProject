Original Tsukune

Dish

Tsukune are Japanese chicken meat balls are popular at Yakitori restaurants. Here they are shaped like small hamburger steaks and cooked in a frying pan. Tsukune is a great appetizer as well as a main dish for everyday dinners.

Sauce Recipe:

Sauce Ingredients

1 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
2 Tbsp Mirin
3 Tbsp water
1/2 tsp Katakuriko
